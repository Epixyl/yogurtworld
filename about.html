About Us

What makes us special?

We make all our frozen yogurt in house with natural, fresh ingredients.

We also compost any ingredients that are unused, such as strawberry leaves.

We seek to meet all dietary needs. We have various options for those with certain dietary restrictions. Some options include vegan, lactose free, sugar free, and dairy free.
