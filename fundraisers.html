Host Your Own Fundraiser!

Step 1:

Send a text message to the owner, Michael Tran, at (858) 452-8889 to schedule a date for your fundraiser.

Step 2:

Create your own flyer for the fundraiser. Both digital and physical copies of the flyer are accepted. Upon presentation of the flyer, customers receive a 10% discount and 20% of the purchase will be donated to the organization. No flyering is allowed inside or directly in front of the store.

Step 3:

Checks will be available usually within 2 days from the date of the fundraiser.
